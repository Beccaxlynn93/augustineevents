About Becca

Your partner in creating unforgettable moments

Hi! I've been a professional events planner and violinist for over 15 years. I specialize in weddings, parties, and corporate events — bringing together live music and beautiful decor to craft experiences your guests will never forget.

Whether you're dreaming of a sweeping ceremony score or a fully styled reception space, I'm here to make it happen with care and intention.

Based in the greater Nashville area, I work closely with each couple and client to understand their vision and bring it to life in a way that feels personal, elegant, and truly one of a kind.
