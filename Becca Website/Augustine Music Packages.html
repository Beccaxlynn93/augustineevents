We are truly honored to be a part of your wedding celebration. Music has the power to create unforgettable memories, and we are excited to contribute to the magic of your big day. Whether it's a romantic ceremony, an upbeat cocktail hour, or everything in between, we look forward to making your wedding day even more memorable with the perfect soundtrack.


About Us

At Augustine Music, we specialize in creating unforgettable musical experiences for weddings tailored to each couple’s unique style and vision. Our mission is to provide personalized, high-quality live music that enhances every moment of your special day, from the ceremony to the reception.


Music Options

Wedding Ceremony

One of the unique features of our service is our commitment to learning three specific songs chosen by you. This ensures that your ceremony feels deeply personal and reflective of your journey as a couple. No request is too big or small!

  • Prelude - 20-30 minutes of music while your guests are being seated

  • Processional - Bridal Party and Bride Entrance

  • Special Song/Unity Song - Optional

  • Recessional - Grand Exit

Cocktail Hour

  • 1 full hour of couples choice of genre (Classical, Pop/Rock, Disney, etc.)
