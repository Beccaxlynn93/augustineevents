Music Options

Wedding Ceremony

One of the unique features of our service is our commitment to learning three specific songs chosen by you. This ensures that your ceremony feels deeply personal and reflective of your journey as a couple. No request is too big or small!

  • Prelude — 20–30 minutes of music while your guests are being seated
  • Processional — Bridal Party and Bride Entrance
  • Special Song / Unity Song — Optional
  • Recessional — Grand Exit

Cocktail Hour

The perfect backdrop for mingling and celebrating after the ceremony.

  • 1 full hour of music in your couple's choice of genre
  • Classical, Pop/Rock, Disney, and more
